Home made ice!

 


Looking for a healthy low calorie idea for lunch, don't worry I have got you covered


What do we need?

  • Water
  • Ice cube trays
  • Freeze or a house in antartica

How do we do it?

  1. Add your water to the ice cube trays
  2. If you live in the arctic simply leave the tray outside for a few hours
  3. If you live in australia then place the ice cube tray in the freezer for a few hours
  4. Enjoy! This also can be served with mustard for an extra kick!
