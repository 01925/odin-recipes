Home made boiling water!

 


So you've decided on a healthy lunch of boiling water? Well look no further here it is!


What do we need?

  • Water
  • A large cooking pot

How do we do it?

  1. Add your water to the cooking pot
  2. Using the standard telekinesis that every human has, imagine the water is boiling
  3. Repeat the previous step until the water is boiled
  4. Once the water has cooled down, you might want to check the other recipe on how to make ice!
