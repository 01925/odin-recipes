Elephant Stew!

 


How to make elephant stew?

How have you ended up here, at this point in life you really should be looking for something a little less horrific to eat for dinner, but who am I to judege you? Let's get cooking!


What do we need?

  • Elephant (This is not going to be easy to source)
  • Lack of conscience, you've just killed an elephant bro.
  • Chainsaw to cut the meat
  • A very good solicitor as you're likely to be going to jail
  • Oxo cubes and chicken stock
  • A realllllllly big cooking pot
  • Several gallons of 5W-40 Oil

How do we do it?

  1. Get your elephant
  2. Using the chainsaw chop your meat to desired size
  3. Go and take a cold shower to comprehend the monsterous actions recently undertaken
  4. Add some 5W-40 oil to the cooking pot and set heat to medium
  5. Brown off the meat and then add the Oxo cubes and chicken stock
  6. Cook for 3-4 weeks
  7. Wait for the police to arrive and arrest you for being a monster
